# Analyzing the Impact of Car Features on Price and Profitability

## Project Overview

The automotive industry is experiencing significant transformation driven by technological innovation, environmental sustainability, and evolving consumer preferences. Manufacturers must continuously optimize vehicle features, pricing strategies, and market positioning to remain competitive while maintaining profitability.

This project investigates how vehicle characteristics such as engine performance, fuel type, market category, and vehicle style influence pricing and profitability. Using data analytics techniques including exploratory data analysis, correlation analysis, regression modeling, and dashboard development, the study aims to identify key drivers of vehicle value and support strategic business decision-making.

---

## Business Problem

Automotive manufacturers face the challenge of balancing customer expectations with production costs and profitability. Understanding which vehicle features contribute most significantly to pricing and consumer demand can help manufacturers:

* Optimize pricing strategies.
* Improve product development decisions.
* Increase profitability.
* Better align vehicle offerings with market demand.

The primary objective of this project is to determine how vehicle specifications and market categories impact Manufacturer Suggested Retail Price (MSRP) and overall profitability.

## Analysis and Findings

### Vehicle Pricing Analysis

The dataset revealed substantial variation in vehicle pricing across market categories, manufacturers, and performance specifications.

#### Key Finding

Premium and luxury vehicles command significantly higher prices; however, higher prices do not always translate into higher profitability.

---

### Fuel Type Analysis

The analysis examined the impact of fuel type on vehicle popularity and market positioning.

#### Key Finding

Hybrid and electric vehicles demonstrate growing market demand, while traditional gasoline-powered vehicles continue to dominate overall market share.

---

### Engine Performance and Price

A scatter plot analysis was conducted to evaluate the relationship between engine horsepower and MSRP.

#### Key Finding

A strong positive relationship exists between engine power and vehicle price, indicating that performance remains an important value driver for consumers.

---

### Market Category Profitability

Vehicle categories were compared based on pricing and profitability indicators.

#### Key Finding

Mid-range SUVs demonstrated the strongest balance between consumer demand and profitability.

---

### Feature Importance Analysis

Regression analysis identified the most influential variables affecting vehicle pricing.

#### Key Finding

The strongest contributors to MSRP include:

* Engine Horsepower
* Market Category
* Vehicle Size
* Fuel Type
* Transmission Configuration

---

## Business Insights

The analysis generated several important insights:

* SUVs represent one of the most profitable vehicle segments.
* Fuel-efficient vehicles continue to gain market acceptance.
* Vehicle performance remains a major pricing determinant.
* Premium pricing strategies are most effective when supported by advanced features and brand value.
* Consumer demand increasingly favors sustainability and technology integration.

---

## Strategic Recommendations

### 1. Focus on Mid-Range SUVs

This segment demonstrates strong demand and attractive profit margins.

### 2. Expand Hybrid and Electric Offerings

Growing environmental awareness and regulatory pressures are increasing demand for alternative fuel vehicles.

### 3. Implement Feature-Based Pricing

Pricing should reflect the value generated by performance, efficiency, and technology features.

### 4. Improve Production Efficiency

Reducing production costs for high-performance vehicles can improve profitability.

### 5. Align Product Development with Consumer Trends

Future vehicle development should prioritize:

* Fuel Efficiency
* Safety Technologies
* Infotainment Systems
* Sustainable Design
